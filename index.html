Solutions

Key to a TB-Free Future

Public Awareness

Spreading knowledge about TB symptoms and cough patterns helps communities seek early testing. Awareness remains the first step in breaking the chain of infection.

AI-Powered Detection

Our system uses machine learning to analyze cough sounds, helping detect potential TB infections faster and more accurately than traditional methods.

Accessible Screening

Designed for mobile and rural clinics, our system makes TB screening accessible even in underserved areas across the Philippines.

Community Health

Partnering with local health centers, we empower communities to use AI tools for monitoring, early intervention, and follow-up treatment of TB cases.

Our commitment to fighting tuberculosis is paving the way for a healthier, stronger Philippines.
